Explore the Universe

Introduction

Explore the Universe is a web-based collection of digital interactives and videos highlighting the latest developments in astronomy and Earth science.

Videos

Explore the Universe videos feature the latest imagery from satellites and space telescopes, and provide unique insight into our understanding of the universe..

Interactives

Interactives allow you to explore objects and materials from different perspectives, and discover how we can combine information to better understand the universe.

Resources

Our website aims to provide you with interesting and valuable cosmic resources. Please feel free to browse our website and explore the mysteries and beauty of the universe!
